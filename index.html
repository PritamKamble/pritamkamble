🏁 Placement Pipeline (How you get hired)

1) Milestones

Finish core weeks, publish 1 capstone, 60/150 DSA problems, resume v2.

2) Mock Interviews

Weekly mocks (DSA + project + HR). Score ≥ 7/10 to unlock referrals.

3) Referrals & Drives

Targeted referrals (when eligible) + curated off‑campus drive list.

4) Applications & Tracking

Apply to 10 roles/week with ATS‑ready resume; track outcomes.

Note: No one can promise a job, but this pipeline maximizes your chances—if you consistently hit the milestones.
